# Jacobi Landing Page — Copy Specification

**Tone**: Authoritative, precise, slightly ominous. Black Mirror meets Consumer Reports.
**Core principle**: Every sentence makes the reader think "wait, they can DO that?"

## HOW IT WORKS — Step Cards

### Step 01
- **Title**: `Submit your target`
- **Desc**: `Drop any product, flight, or booking URL into the scanner. If it has a price tag, Jacobi can find its real range.`
- **Icon**: Search
- **Rationale**: "Submit your target" reframes the URL as an investigation subject. "Real range" hints at the hidden price spectrum.

### Step 02
- **Title**: `Swarm launches`
- **Desc**: `24 agent profiles disperse across four discrimination axes — location, device, cookies, referrer — hitting the URL in coordinated waves.`
- **Icon**: Network
- **Rationale**: "Swarm" and "disperse" suggest military precision. "Coordinated waves" implies strategy, not randomness.

### Step 03
- **Title**: `Patterns emerge`
- **Desc**: `Every response cross-referenced. Statistical outliers become evidence. Pricing bias becomes readable data.`
- **Icon**: Cpu
- **Rationale**: Three short sentences in present tense. Feels like real-time detection. "Pricing bias" names the crime.

### Step 04
- **Title**: `Read the verdict`
- **Desc**: `A plain-English breakdown of what you'd save with a different profile — and exactly which vector was used to overcharge you.`
- **Icon**: Shield
- **Rationale**: "Verdict" (not "report") implies a judgment has been made. "Which vector was used against you" personalizes the harm.
