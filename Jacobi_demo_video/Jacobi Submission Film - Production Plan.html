Document 03

3D & WebGL animation plan.

Three custom sequences. Built in After Effects or as headless Three.js scenes captured at 4K. Aesthetically continuous with the live site's globe/radial — same colors, same hairlines, same restraint.

SEQ-A · "Fracture grid" (B-01 → B-05, 0:00–0:35)

  • Layer 1: typed URL (mono, animated cursor).
  • Layer 2: fork lines drawn with cobalt 1-px strokes, ease-out 700ms each, staggered.
  • Layer 3: 6×4 tile grid. Tiles render mini-browser chrome, animated price ticker per tile (random pricing noise, top-right tile lands on red $640).
  • Layer 4: zoom + isolate the worst-overcharge tile. Annotations (device · location · referrer) type-on.
  • Layer 5: tile dissolve → particle field → orbital rings (matches landing globe's vibe).
  • Camera: parallax dolly on Z-axis through layers. No spinning. No swooping.

SEQ-B · "Architecture pipeline" (B-11 → B-15, 1:20–2:10)

  • Schematic with 8 hairline-bordered nodes. All cobalt strokes. Background pure ink.
  • Per-edge "packet" pulse: a 3-pixel cobalt dot travels each edge in 600ms with motion-blur trail.
  • Labels render in JetBrains Mono, fade up sync'd to VO (one label per noun beat).
  • Quota node pulses 1.05× when crossed. Stripe↔Supabase webhook arrow uses dashed stroke + back-loop arrow.
  • Transitions to radial deploy via camera-lift: schematic recedes, radial scales up at center.

Document 05

Music & sound design.

One bespoke score in three movements. Tonal palette chosen against the cobalt-on-ink visual identity: cold synth pads, analog sub-bass, mechanical percussion, no orchestral swell, no drops.

0:00 → 0:35Act I · Tension

Movement 1 — Sub-bass + sparse pulse

Atonal sub (40–80Hz). Soft white-noise textures. A single mechanical pulse at 78 BPM enters at 0:08, doubles to 156 BPM at 0:23 when tiles populate. No melody. Ends on a held minor 2nd before Act II impact.

0:35 → 1:20Act II · Arrival

Movement 2 — Cold synth pad + bracket impact

One deep impact on the brand lock (sub + reverb-tail). A frozen analog pad sustains under the VO. Soft "deploy" mech-ticks per identity on the globe. Tempo settles to 84 BPM. Resolve on the thesis line.

1:20 → 2:10Act III · Pipeline

Movement 2b — Granular sequencer + arpeggio

A 16-step muted arpeggio in cobalt key (D minor, Pythagorean tuning). Per-node confirm chime sits on top. Build escalates as 24-identity radial deploys, peaks at 2:00 when report-write happens. Drops to pad-only for the "stored, shareable, yours" tail.

2:10 → 4:25Act IV · Walkthrough

Movement 3 — Confident under-track

Persistent low pad with a slow 4-note ostinato. Sits low in the mix (−18dB beneath VO). Adds micro-percussion under UI clicks. Brief lift at the verdict reveal (3:08) and again at the share-URL copy moment (3:58). No crescendo. The product carries the energy.

4:25 → 4:50Act V · Lift

Movement 3b — Tempo + sub-melody enters

A 3-note serif melody plays once over the buyer grid. Tempo holds 84 BPM. Subtle widening of the stereo field. This is the only "anthem" moment in the film — keep it 4 seconds.

4:50 → 5:00Act VI · Resolve

Movement 4 — Single sustain → silence

Pad collapses to a single sustained note (Dm root). 200ms silence before the logo lock. Final tail dies in the last second. No outro stinger.

Mix targets

  • Dialogue (VO): −12 LUFS short-term · −18 LUFS integrated · always 8–10dB above music bed
  • Music bed: −22 LUFS integrated · sidechained −6dB under VO
  • SFX: per-element peaks −9dBFS, never compete with VO consonants
  • Master: −16 LUFS integrated · −1dBTP ceiling · YouTube / Vimeo / web-safe

SFX library

  • Brand impact — sub + reverb tail, ~1.8s decay
  • Identity deploy tick — short mechanical click, pitched up 24 times over deploy
  • KPI confirm — synth resolve, D5
  • Reticle lock — two-step metal click for zoom-and-isolate
  • Type-on bed — soft mech-keyboard layer, low in mix, under all captions
  • Red flicker sting — used twice only (B-02 + B-22)
